Ye and Nike's parnership continued in 2012, with the release of 2 limited edition colorways of the Air Yeezy 2. The Yeezy 2 pulled inspiration from Ye's favorite Nike/Jordan sneakers, and built on lessons learned from the development of the Yeezy 1. The sneakers were a roaring success, and a third sneaker was released in the "Red October" colorway in 2014.
