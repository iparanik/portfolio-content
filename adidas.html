Adidas x Ye

Skip to Main Content

Ye and Nike's partnership went unrenewed, after Nike was unable to meet Ye's requests for a contract equivalent to a major athelete. As a result, Kanye signed a sneaker deal with Adidas. The first shoe from their collaboration was the Yeezy 350, which was designed to compete with the then popular Nike Roshe Run.
